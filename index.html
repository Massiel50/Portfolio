About Me

I am Massiel, the last of my kind. I was born in Dominican Republic and migrated to the U.S of A very young. I grew up in bronx, New York and lived there the majority of my life. I then moved to Los Angeles and lived there for 7 years. Now I am here in Florida, ready to retire. I have a long ways from that.

I have studied for a bachelors in Computer Information Systems, specializing in software programming. I decided that completing my degree was pointless and not cost efficient. I am a very ambitious person, and to me this is a make it or break it point in my life. That's what I say to motivate myself and put a fire in me.

In my own time, I dont socialize much. Even though I don't, I can make friends very easily. I stay home for the most part, though I do love to sample different types of food, especially asian dining. I eat chocolate ALL the time. My favorite is belgium milk chocolate. Pretty much anything sweet will float my boat. I enjoy yoga, meditation and any calm environment. I also enjoy video games, mostly rpg and simulation games.

For the most part, I go with the flow because life is too short.
